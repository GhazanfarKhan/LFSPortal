About Survey

Labour Force Survey 2020-21 (District level)

Labour Force Survey 2020-21 System for data collection, House listing and Supervsior Dashboard make it possible for enumerator to take survey and send data in minutes of hours.This survey also provides the platform towrads Automated Survey Management System an ERP Solution Developed by SS Team DP Center. Labour force survey has different indicators about the labour working in the industry,labour conditions, literacy rate,demographic information ,underemployment,employment and unemployment.
